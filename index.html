About Austin

After graduating from Michigan State University with a degree in Media and Information, I packed up my Jeep Wrangler and headed west to California, where I received my Master of Fine Arts in Screenwriting from California State University, Fullerton.

After several years in the entertainment industry, I decided to expand my horizons and further my knowledge about coding and advanced programming languages. Technology has always been a big part of my life, from my love of video games to building several computers on my own, and the prospect of constructing web sites and software as part of a team of great programmers is at the core of why I dove head-first into learning how to code.

I also have several interests outside of development:

  • Comic Books
  • Film & Television
